    About SurplusSmiles

    SurplusSmiles is a platform that connects restaurants with surplus food to NGOs that can distribute it to those in need. Together, we can reduce food waste and fight hunger.

    How It Works

    1. Register

    Restaurants and NGOs sign up on our platform.

    2. Donate

    Restaurants donate surplus food at closing time.

    3. Collect

    NGOs collect and distribute the food to those in need.
