Join Us in Reducing Food Waste!

Our mission is to connect restaurants with local NGOs to donate leftover food and help those in need.

How It Works

  1. Restaurants register with our app.
  2. Receive notifications about leftover food.
  3. Donate food to local NGOs or shelters.
  4. Help feed those in need in your community!
